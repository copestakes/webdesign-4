  • Easy access to the venue for Firefly pass holders
  • Access to showers, restrooms, food vendors, and more
  • Access to Ranger Stations offering information, restrooms, water, charging, waste drop off and more
  • Free waste collection bags upon arrival (more available at Ranger Stations)

    • Coveniences & Amenities

    The Shower House

    At Firefly, camping means cozy. Leave the wet wipes at home - Firefly’s permanent shower facilities in North Camping includes a space with wall-to-wall mirrors and power outlets and in South camping, you’ll find air-conditioned showers. Both offer a next level festival experience for purchase to let you comfortably get ready and feel fresh for a weekend of unforgettable music and experiences.

    General Store

    Forget something? No worries! Our campground General Stores offer camping gear, toiletries,
    batteries, ice, water, food and so much more.

    Ranger Stations

    The campgrounds have Ranger Stations around every corner offering information, restrooms, water, charging and more! Ranger Stations are manned by our awesome festival Rangers who are your go-to people for anything you need during your festival weekend.

    Shuttles

    During the festival, Firefly offers free shuttles to help you move between camping lots and parking areas. Camping shuttle routes and schedules will be posted closer to the festival.

    What to Pack

    • Tent
    • Pillow, sleeping bag and sleeping pad
    • Camping gear
    • Shelter, tarp or tapestry for shade
    • Camping chairs and tables
    • Reusable water bottles (refill stations available)
    • Cooler Sunscreen and bug repellent
    • Headlamp or flashlight
    • Lantern
    • Sunglasses
    • Rain gear
    • Towel
    • Hat
    • Bandana
    • Jacket or hoodie
    • Extra sneakers / boots
    • Toiletries (toothpaste, deodorant, etc)
    • Band-aids and first aid kit
    • A clear backpack
    • Biodegradable plastic bags for trash (we provide some bags for trash and recycling, but extras are nice!)
    • Extra set of keys to keep with you at all times
    • Photo ID
    • Flag / balloon / other way to identify your campsite
